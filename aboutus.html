what is matager

Matager offers a personalized e-commerce platform designed to kickstart your online selling journey with ease, enabling you to start generating more income by selling your products directly on your own website. Our solution helps you build your brand and reach customers effortlessly, providing a professional, low-cost alternative to traditional online stores.

what is matager solve

Our solution addresses the challenges faced by small stores, where the high cost of creating an online store often becomes a barrier. Matager offers an online store starting from just 500 EGP per month, providing an affordable and fair pricing option for small businesses. Plus, the store can easily upgrade its plan as it grows, ensuring that your e-commerce platform scales with your success.

what the idea come from

Recognizing this gap, Matager was born from an idea conceived 4 years ago, aimed at providing affordable and professional online store solutions. Over the past 2 years, our team has diligently developed and refined the platform to ensure it meets the needs of businesses looking to build their brand and reach customers without breaking the bank.
