How to use Matager?

Reading time: 5 mins | Published 11/09/2023 | Level: Intermediate

Getting Started

Follow these simple 5 steps to get started:

  1. 1-Visit our pricing page to explore available plans.
    2. The price and features of plans may change from time to time. We recommend reviewing the details before making a selection.
  2. 2-Select the plan that best suits your needs.
  3. 3-Click on the Get Started button.
  4. 4-Fill out the form and hit Submit.
    5. Please ensure you have entered a valid phone number and email address, as we will contact you and deliver your store based on this information.
  5. 5-Your request will be sent to our team. Please allow 24 to 48 hours for your site to be completed and deployed.

Images Cloud

Follow these simple 5 steps to set up your Images Cloud:

  1. 1. Visit Imgur to get started.
    2. Please note that the features and pricing of Imgur plans may change over time. Review the details carefully before proceeding.
  2. 2. Create an Imgur account using Google or any other preferred method.
  3. 3. After logging in, you will be redirected to the Imgur API page.
  4. 4. Register your application using the Postman callback URL: https://www.getpostman.com/oauth2/callback.
  5. 5. Fill out the required form and click Submit.
  6. 6. Copy the client ID displayed after successfully creating the application.
    7. If you lose access to the page, go to Imgur Settings to retrieve your client ID.
  7. 7. Paste the client ID into the "Image Cloud ID" input field in the Getting Started form.

Website Customization

  1. Personalized Store Setup: We will contact you to customize your store's main visuals, such as the banner image, and set up additional details like free shipping options, promotional banners, and more to reflect your brand identity.
  2. Comprehensive Documentation: Our detailed guides will walk you through updating your store information, including product details, categories, and store policies. With step-by-step instructions, you'll quickly learn how to navigate the system and manage your store effortlessly.
  3. Thorough Testing and Seamless Launch: Once the setup is complete, we will rigorously test your website to ensure everything works seamlessly before launch, giving you peace of mind.
  4. Ongoing Support and Updates: Enjoy regular system updates and expert support to help you keep your store running smoothly and adapt to new features.
